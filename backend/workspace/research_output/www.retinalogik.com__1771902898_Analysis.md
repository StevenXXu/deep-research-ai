# One-Liner
RetinaLogik offers a portable, AI-powered VR headset and software platform designed to streamline and enhance vision testing in eye care clinics.

## 1. Problem & Solution
**Problem:** Traditional visual field testing methods are cumbersome, require dedicated space and trained technicians, can be uncomfortable for patients (leading to unreliable results), and often lack efficient integration with Electronic Medical Records (EMR).

**Solution:** RetinaLogik provides a compact, patient-friendly VR headset (RVF200) that performs multiple core eye exams (visual field, color vision, acuity) with AI-powered analysis and prompts in 35+ languages. The platform streamlines clinic workflows, enhances patient comfort, integrates with EMR systems, and offers improved accessibility across diverse patient demographics (pediatric to aging).

## 2. Market Opportunity
**Target Customer:** Optometrists and ophthalmologists seeking to improve clinic efficiency, patient experience, and diagnostic accuracy. The device also targets hospitals and large clinics who need the space savings.

## 3. Product Deep Dive

BookWell AI aims to provide a fully functional AI Finance Workforce [1]. The core offering centers around automating pre-accounting operations, particularly for SMEs and accounting firms [3].

*   **Features:**
    *   **Automated Bookkeeping:** Automates document collection and processing, reducing manual data entry [19, 20].
    *   **Invoice Processing:** Automates compliance with real-time ABN & GST validation [18].
    *   **Financial Reporting:** Provides actionable insights into client needs and pricing strategies [19, 20].
    *   **Document Organization:** Automatically identifies and organizes bills, invoices, and bank statements [2].
    *   **Email Integration:** Scans inbox every 15 minutes to capture forwarded documents [2].
    *   **AI Learning:** Studies company's transaction history and industry trends [2].
    *   **Compliance:** ABN & GST validation [18, 20].

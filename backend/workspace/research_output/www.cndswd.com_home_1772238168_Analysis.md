**Market Sizing Considerations (TAM/SAM/SOM)**

To accurately assess the potential market opportunity for CNDSWD, it's crucial to define the Total Addressable Market (TAM), Serviceable Available Market (SAM), and Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) [48, 49].

*   **TAM:** The total market demand for AEC software solutions globally or within China [48, 49].
*   **SAM:** The portion of the TAM that CNDSWD can realistically serve with its current products and capabilities [48, 49].
*   **SOM:** The portion of the SAM that CNDSWD can realistically capture in the short to medium term, considering competition and market conditions [48, 49].

For example, if CNDSWD has a 5% SOM and their average client brings in $1,000:

`SOM = (Total Potential Customers) * (Penetration Rate) * (Average Revenue Per Customer)`

`SOM = 1,000,000 potential clients * 0.2% penetration * $500`

`SOM = $10,000,000` [51]
